Czechs who left a mark in the World history

There are only ten million people inhabiting Czech Republic these days, and it always was a very small country. This tiny nation, however, has greatly contributed to the European and World civilization. Among many others, there were three men, who made a special, lasting impact on the World we live in:
Jan Hus
Jan Komensky
Vaclav Havel
